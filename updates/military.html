Military Discount

We are eternally grateful for the hard work and sacrifices made by America's armed forces.
As a small way to express our gratitude, we proudly offer VIP pricing to all active-duty military, retired military, and veterans.
Verify I.D.
Simply visit any P.C. Richard & Son store and present the manager with your valid military or veteran I.D.
Shop & Save
You'll receive a discount in the form of either a percentage off your purchase, or a gift card reimbursement for a portion of the purchase price.
*This discount must be applied by a manager in-store, and cannot be applied to an online purchase.
While some exclusions do apply due to manufacturer restrictions, we will do everything possible to provide you with a discount on your purchase with us.
