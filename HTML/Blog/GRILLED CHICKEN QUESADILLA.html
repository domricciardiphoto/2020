Grilled Chicken Quesadilla with grill veggies prepared using the Weber Grill Basket and the Weber Smoke Fire. This recipe is quick and easy.
  • YIELD : 20
  • SKILL LEVEL : Beginner
  • COST : $
  • PREP TIME : 20 minutes
  • GRILLING TIME : 12 - 15 minutes
INGREDIENTS:
  • 6 each 8oz Chicken Breasts
  • Olive Oil / Canola Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Weber seasoning (Kicking Chicken or Garlic & Herb)

Chicken Taco Seasoning
  • 1 1/2 cups of chili powder
  • 1 tbsp of onion powder
  • 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp of cayenne
  • 1 tbsp of red pepper flakes
  • 2 tbsp of salt
  • 1 1/2 tbsp of pepper
  • 3 Red bell peppers
  • 3 Green bell peppers
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 2 pounds of Monterey Jack shredded
  • 2 pounds of Mild Cheddar shredded
  • 40 6" Flour Tortillas
GAS/CHARCOAL INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Set Temp on Smoke Fire Unit to 525°F. Once grill is properly preheated, use your grill brush to clean the grill grates. Place the grill basket on the grill to heat up. Charcoal temp range 450 – 550, set up two zone fire direct & indirect. Gas temp range 450 – 550 set up direct for grilling the chicken breast and turn one burner off for indirect to finish up the quesadilla.
  2. Take the veggies slice them up 1/8 in thick for fajitas and lightly oil, salt & pepper them and place to the side.
  3. Take the chicken taco seasonings and mix them together in a small bowl and set them aside for seasoning the chicken breast.
  4. Take chicken breast and remove any excess fat and the tenderloin. Using the plastic wrap place the chicken breast between the plastic wrap skin side down, and gently pound the chicken until it is fairly flat. Lightly coat the chicken breasts with oil and then season the chicken with the Chicken Taco Seasoning.
  5. Place the chicken on the grill skin side down. After the chicken is placed on the grill, pour the vegetables in the grill pan. Grill the chicken for 4-5 minutes a side and move the veggies around when you flip the chicken. Once the chicken is fully cooked (internal temp 165 degrees) remove it and the veggies from the grill and allow them to rest for 3-5 minutes. During this time get your tortillas & cheese together to build your quesadilla. Turn your grill temp down to 400 degrees.
  6. Slice your chicken breast in thin slices and rough chop your grilled veggie. Build your quesadilla by laying out a flour tortilla and layering it with cheese, chicken, & grilled veggies and then topping it with another flour tortilla. Place the quesadilla back on the grill for about 2 minutes a side. Slice into 4 triangles and serve
SMOKE FIRE INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Follow the directions listed above.
