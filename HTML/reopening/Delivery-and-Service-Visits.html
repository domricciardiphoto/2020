Delivery, Installations and Service Visits
Our coronavirus response to keep your health and safety first
An Adult over the age of 18 must be present to accept and sign for the delivery.
We have a plan
In accordance with CDC guidelines, we have developed and trained our delivery, installation and service teams on new health and safety protocols designed to keep everyone protected.
What We Will Do:
In-Home Visit:
Our Associates will:
  • Perform a personal health checks prior to beginning the work day
  • Let you know we are on our way so you can prepare for our arrival
  • Wear protective masks and gloves
  • Clean and disinfect our equipment and your products

Contactless Delivery:
Our Associates will:
  • Perform a personal health checks prior to beginning the work day
  • Let you know we are on our way so you can prepare for our arrival
  • Place product on your doorstep, in an open garage, or on the curb in front of apartment building
What You Should Do:
In-Home Visit:
  • An adult over the age of 18 must be present to accept delivery. A valid photo ID and credit card matching the name of the purchaser is required at the time of delivery
  • We recommend everyone in household wear protective coverings while we are there
  • If possible, increase ventilation to the work area by opening a window.
  • As an added precaution, we recommend that you also clean areas of contact again after the team has departed

Contactless Delivery:
  • An adult over the age of 18 must be present to accept delivery. A valid photo ID and credit card matching the name of the purchaser is required at the time of delivery
  • If you selected "haul-away" at checkout, any items to be removed must be outside of your home, on the curb outside an apartment building, or in an open garage.
