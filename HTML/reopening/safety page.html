How to order?

Delivery, Installation and Service
For the health and safety of our delivery and service teams, as well as our entire customer base, we have temporarily altered our standard procedures all delivery, installation, and service appointments:

Please call us prior to your scheduled date if someone in your household:

  • Has been diagnosed with the coronavirus (COVID-19)
  • Is currently quarantined due to potential exposure to coronavirus (COVID-19)
  • Is exhibiting symptoms such as a cough, fever, or flu-like symptoms.

Please do the following for the safety of our employees:
  • Practice appropriate Social Distancing while the delivery team or technician is in your home
  • Wipe the surfaces of any appliances being serviced or removed with sanitizing wires or solution.

If for any reason you are unable to comply with these best practices, we may be required required to change your delivery, installation or service appointment to a future date. Our employees have been advised to follow additional health and safety protocols, including frequently washing their hands, abiding by a "no handshake" policy, and encouraging social distancing. We are monitoring any potential employee health issues and are acting with an abundance of caution to protect our employees and customers. We are continuously monitoring all communications about novel coronavirus (COVID-19) from the CDC and the WHO, implementing guidelines and best practices as they are issued.
"Contactless" Doorstep Delivery
Our “Contactless” Doorstep delivery includes placing of products on your doorstep, in an open garage, or on the curb in front of apartment buildings. A valid photo ID and credit card matching the name of the purchaser is required at the time of delivery. We will take away your old disconnected product, provided you have agreed to this at checkout and product has been left outside of your home.
In-Home Delivery
Appliances delivered in-home will be unboxed, leveled and placed in the room of your choice. TVs, mattresses and other products delivered in-home will be brought to the room of your choice. We will take away your old disconnected product, provided you have agreed to this at checkout. A valid photo ID and credit card matching the name of the purchaser is required at the time of delivery. Our teams will be operating under new safety protocols.
In-Home Installation & Service
We have fully reopened our in-home installation and service offerings and our teams are currently operating under new safety protocols. To schedule an installation or service appointment call your local store, or you can reach our customer service department at 1-800-696-2000.
