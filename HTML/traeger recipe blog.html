• DIFFICULTY : 1/5
  • PREP TIME : 5 Mins
  • COOK TIME : 15-20 Mins
  • SERVES : 4-6
  • HARDWOOD : CHERRY
INGREDIENTS:
  • 4 THICK CUT PORK CHOPS
  • TRAEGER PORK AND POULTRY RUB
  • TRAEGER APRICOT BBQ SAUCE
PREPARATION:
  1. 1 When ready to cook, set the temperature to High and preheat, lid closed for 15 minutes.
  2. 2 Season the pork chops liberally with Traeger Pork & Poultry rub.
  3. 3 Place chops directly on the grill grate and cook for 7-10 minutes on each side until grill marks develop and the internal temperature reaches 145°F when an instant read thermometer is inserted into the thickest part.
  4. 4 During the last 5 minutes of cooking, brush the chops with Traeger Apricot BBQ Sauce and cook until they come up to temperature and the sauce is set.
  5. 5 Let rest 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
  6. 6 *Cook times will vary depending on set and ambient temperatures.
