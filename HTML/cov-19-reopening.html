Note: All locations offer curbside pick-up. In-store shopping available at select locations only. Use our store locator to find out more.
We have taken guidance from the CDC in the development of our of new health and safety protocols. These new policies promote regular cleaning and disinfecting procedures for highly touched surfaces. Our sales teams are working hard every day to provide customers with a safe and comfortable shopping experience. Store Hours
What other steps are we taking:
  • Providing employees with high quality masks and gloves while at work
  • Requiring the use of masks for all customers and providing gloves upon request.
  • Limiting the amount of customers in the store at a time to maintain a 1-to-1 relationship between sales person and customer
  • Encouraging social distancing with the use of overhead audio messages, floor and wall signage. Our sales and management team will also be actively monitoring traffic throughout the store.
  • Providing additional protective equipment, such as disposable mattress coverings, wherever possible
  • Strongly advising the use of credit cards and contactless payment methods, though we will still accept cash payments where necessary.
Customer appointments

Doorstep and in-home delivery, installation and service
In accordance with CDC guidelines, we have developed and trained our delivery, installation and service teams on new health and safety protocols designed to keep everyone protected when we visit our customer’s homes. Our new policies include specific actions taken by our teams to promote cleanliness and improve communication with our customers before, during and after a visit to their home.
