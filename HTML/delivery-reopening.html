Our coronavirus response to keep your health and safety first
A letter from Gregg Richard, President & CEO to our customers Click here
We have a plan
In accordance with CDC guidelines, we have developed and trained our delivery, installation and service teams on new health and safety protocols designed to keep everyone protected.
Before We Arrive:
  • We will call you ahead of time to confirm your delivery, installation or service appointment, review our safety procedure and answer any questions you might have.
  • Our delivery teams perform personal health checks ever morning and are prohibited from working if they feel ill in any way.
  • We clean and disinfect all equipment
  • We restock vehicle with masks, gloves and disinfectant
During our Visit:
What we will do…
  • Put on protective gear before we enter your home. Including masks and gloves.
  • Keep a 6-foot distance from you as we enter and for the remainder of our visit
  • Clean and disinfect your product, as well as all surfaces that we have touched
  • Once the job is complete, we'll check that your product is working and answer any questions you may have.

What you should do…
  • We recommend everyone in household wear protective coverings while we are there
  • If possible, increase ventilation to the work area by opening a window.
After we Leave:
What we will do…
  • Properly dispose of all safety supplies and trash
  • Clean and disinfect all equipment
  • Clean and disinfect commonly touched surfaces in our vehicles

What you should do…
  • As an added precaution, we recommend that you also clean areas of contact after the team has departed
