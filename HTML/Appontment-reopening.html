In-Store Customer Appointments, What To Expect
Our coronavirus response to keep your health and safety first
A letter from Gregg Richard, President & CEO to our customers Click here
We have a plan
In an effort to keep our customers and our sales team safe, while still providing the same great in-store service, we will be offering the ability to shop by appointment at any location where it has been deemed safe by state and local governments and our team to open our doors to the public. We have new procedures in place in provide you with a dedicated time and sales representative to answer all your questions in a safe way.
Schedule your in-store appointment here. In-store appointments are only available at select stores, use our store locator to find a location near you.
Before You Arrive:
What we will do…
  • We will call you to confirm your appointment time, let you know where to meet us when you arrive and answer any questions you might have.
  • Our delivery teams perform personal health checks ever morning and are prohibited from working if they feel ill in any way.
  • Our sales teams performs personal health checks ever morning and are prohibited from working if they feel ill in any way.
  • We will clean and disinfect sales terminals and highly touched surfaces.
During Your Visit:
What we will do…
  • Wear protective gear, including masks and gloves while we are in the showroom.
  • Meet you at the front of the store to welcome you while maintaining appropriate social distance
  • Keep a 6-foot distance from you, as well as other customers and employees, as we move throughout the showroom
  • Offer safe contactless payment options to complete you purchase and assist you in bringing items out to your car.

What you should do…
  • Upon arrival, wait at the front of showroom in be greeted by our showroom manager or sales team.
  • We recommend everyone in your party wear protective masks and gloves while you are in the showroom. If you did not bring your own we will provide them for you.
  • Be aware of posted signs and overhead audio messages indicating safety protocols to be followed while in the showroom.
After you Leave:
What we will do…
  • Properly dispose of all soiled cleaning supplies, protective equipment and trash
  • Clean and disinfect sales terminals
  • Clean and disinfect commonly touched surfaces

What you should do…
  • As an added precaution, we recommend that you also wash your hands after departing from the showroom.
