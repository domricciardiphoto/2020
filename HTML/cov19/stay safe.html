How to order?
  1. Most retail locations in New York, New Jersey, Connecticut and Pennsylvania Stores are OPEN and operating via a "contactless" shopping experience. Customers are not permitted in the store, however items can be purchased over the phone and will be placed in your vehicle. Please see our Store Locator for up to date store hours.
  2. You can order on pcrichard.com
    • • Select contactless Curbside Pick-Up for your local store or call your local store to place an order
    • • Have the item delivered to your home.
  3. Call 1-877-727-1909 to place your order.
Deliveries & Shipping
  1. Appliance Delivery via P.C. Richard & Son truck (Tuesday-Saturday) –Appliances will be delivered, unboxed and placed on your doorstep, in an open garage, or on the curb in front of apartment buildings. We will take away your old disconnected appliance, only if it is outside the house or apartment building. Due to health and safety concerns, our delivery teams will not be entering your house or apartment building. A valid photo ID and credit card matching the name of the purchaser is required at the time of delivery.
  2. TV Delivery via P. C. Richard & Son truck (Tuesday-Saturday) – TVs will be delivered to your doorstep, in an open garage, or on the curb in front of apartment buildings. A valid photo ID and credit card matching the name of the purchaser is required at the time of delivery.
  3. Appliances Delivery out of P.C. Richard & Son delivery area – procedures remain unchanged. You will be contacted by Pilot Air for delivery date and item will be delivered to your doorstep.
  4. Parcel – Any items ordered with a UPS or USPS delivery method will be delivered in accordance with the current procedures of those carriers.

Service
For the health and safety of our delivery and service teams, as well as our entire customer base, we have temporarily altered our standard procedures for appliance service calls:

Please call us prior to your scheduled date if someone in your household:
  • • Has been diagnosed with the coronavirus (COVID-19),
  • • Is currently quarantined due to potential exposure to coronavirus (COVID-19)
  • • Is exhibiting symptoms such as a cough, fever, or flu-like symptoms.

Please do the following for the safety of our employees:
  • • Use sanitizing wipes or solution, please wipe the surfaces of the appliance being serviced
  • • Please practice appropriate Social Distancing while the technician is in your home

If for any reason you are unable to comply with these best practices, we may be required to alter your delivery and/or service procedures to a future date
Our employees have been advised to follow additional health and safety protocols, including frequently washing their hands, abiding by a "no handshake" policy, and encouraging social distancing. We are monitoring any potential employee health issues and are acting with an abundance of caution to protect our employees and customers. We are continuously monitoring all communications about novel coronavirus (COVID-19) from the CDC and the WHO, implementing guidelines and best practices as they are issued.
