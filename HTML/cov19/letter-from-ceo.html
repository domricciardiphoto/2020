Letter from Gregg Richard, CEO 3-24-20

To Our Valued Customers,

For 110 years, P.C. Richard & Son has been committed to providing our communities with essential appliances, technology and a world class shopping experience. During these unprecedented times, we have continued this commitment to you, our customers, by meeting the increased demand for freezers, refrigerators, washers, dryers, computers and other home office essentials, necessary for keeping your home lives fully operational.

In order to continue to provide you with these essential products, we are announcing a new way to shop with us in eligible stores, “contactless” Curbside Pick-Up. This service will allow you to pick-up the product you need without requiring you to enter our stores. These new measures will provide added safety for both our customers and employees. For any eligible items ordered on PCRichard.com, follow the instruction on your confirmation email, and we will deliver the product directly to your car while it is parked in our store’s parking lot.

If you have not ordered online in advance and would like to take advantage of this service, we will be more than happy to assist you. When you arrive at the store, please stay in your car. You may then call the store phone number and place an order with a sales representative. Your merchandise will be brought to your trunk without you leaving your car. Please be advised, certain locations will remain closed and will not be offering “contactless” curbside pick-up. Please visit our website for an up-to-date list of eligible stores.

In the interest of providing further safety to our customers and employees, we are also making changes to our Next Day Home Delivery service. Effective March 23rd, for the delivery of large appliances and TVs, we will deliver the product to your doorstep or to an open garage only. Unfortunately, we are unable to bring the product inside your home. We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience that this may cause. We will gladly take your old appliance for proper disposal, if it is outside of your home and has been cleaned.

We are also suspending all product installations, and non-appliance service calls. Should you require assistance rescheduling a previously scheduled delivery, installation, or service call, please call 1-877-727-1909, and we will happy to assist you.

We are continuously monitoring all communications from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention and the World Health Organization and implementing guidelines and best practices as they are issued. As the circumstances of this pandemic change, so too may our plans and operations. We are committed to continuously updating our loyal customers and valued employees as changes in circumstances occur.

The Richard family thanks you for your ongoing loyalty and understanding during these challenging times. Rest assured, we are committed to continuing to provide you with essential appliances and technology equipment so you can remain both safe and connected.

Be safe.

Sincerely,

Gregg Richard
President & CEO
P.C. Richard & Son
