Multiple items are permitted, however Major Appliances, Mattresses and TVs larger than 70” are not applicable for this service. Installations and setups are not available with same day delivery.
Next Day Delivery
Next Day TV and Major Appliance Delivery is available in the local P.C. Richard & Son Delivery Area on all in-stock models when ordered before 5pm, except as noted below:
  • New York and New Jersey – P.C. Richard & Son delivery service is not available on Monday.
  • Connecticut - P.C. Richard & Son delivery service is not available on Wednesday or Sunday.

Recycling Program
P.C. Richard & Son offers a recycling program in the states of New York, New Jersey, Connecticut & Pennsylvania for customers purchasing a replacement appliance from P.C. Richard & Son. The replaced appliances are delivered to a licensed third party recycling company who ensures that the products will be recycled in accordance with all applicable laws.
Shipping Outside the P.C. Richard & Son Local Delivery Area
We ship within the contiguous United States and do not ship internationally. While we take all possible measures to ensure that your order leaves our warehouse in superior condition, damages may occur to items during transit. It is extremely important that you open and inspect your merchandise immediately upon arrival from Pilot Air, our freight shipping partner for orders outside of our delivery area. Damages must be reported to them within 3 business days of your delivery for them to honor and investigate the claim. You can begin the damage claim process here. Please allow 15 calendar days after your claim for the investigation to be completed.

In-Store Pickup
Choose our In-Store Pickup option when adding a product to your shopping cart or during the checkout process. During checkout you will be prompted to select a primary showroom location followed up by up to 5 more secondary showrooms based on the zip code you have entered.

Please Note:

For your security, the person picking up an in-store order must be the person named on the purchasing credit card. Only the cardholder or an authorized user on the credit card account may pick up items in store. An authorized cardholder is a cardholder with the same account number as the credit card used for the purchase and has the same last name as the cardholder.

The following is required for all In-Store Pickups:
  1. 1. A state issued photo ID or driver's license in the name of the purchaser.
  2. 2. The credit card used for the purchase.
  3. 3. Your e-mail confirmation or order number.


The dollar amount of your order will be withheld from your available credit card balance until the order has been completed.
NOTE: Do not go to the store to pick up the items until you receive a "Merchandise Ready" e-mail from us.
If you have any questions or concerns, contact us at (877) 727-1909 or via e-mail.
